Tips and Tricks

Protecting yourself from scams

Watch out for scammers who try to take advantage of coronavirus (COVID-19). Scams can take many forms. Be wary of offers to assist you access your superannuation or high-return investment opportunities.

Track your spending

To see how much money is going out, track what you spend over one or two weeks. Include every transaction, no matter how small. See track your spending for easy ways to do this. This will give you a clear view of where your money is going.
